Merging Processes at EverSure

A Business Process Management case study on merging traditional and digital insurance sales processes.

EverSure has been providing insurance to businesses for more than 20 years. After acquiring a digital competitor, they need to merge two different processes for the same service.

Traditional Process Strengths
  • Higher contract value (€20,000 vs €500)
  • Better cycle time efficiency (8.86% vs 6.33%)

  • Automated lead generation and filtering

Process Analysis

Cycle Time Efficiency

Both processes have low cycle time efficiency, indicating significant waiting times compared to processing times. The traditional process has slightly higher efficiency (8.86% vs 6.33%) but longer overall cycle time.

Processing vs Waiting Time

The traditional process has more processing time (21h vs 10.27h) and waiting time (216h vs 152h). The digital process is faster overall but slightly less efficient.

Conversion Rates

The traditional process has higher conversion rates at each stage, while the digital process suffers from high lead filtering (50%) and non-response rates (70%).

Resource Costs

Traditional process resources cost more per hour (€15-40/h) compared to digital process resources (€10-20/h), but generate higher-value contracts.

Identified Issues

Issue 1
Inefficient Information Systems Integration

Multiple disconnected systems across business units:

  • Traditional: CNTR and OPER systems
  • Digital: INSU and AUIN systems
Impact:
  • Higher error rates in digital process (15% vs 5%)
  • Duplicate customer information
  • Increased training requirements
  • Missed cross-selling opportunities
  • Higher IT costs
Issue 2
Excessive Waiting Times in Traditional Process

Significant waiting times at multiple stages:

  • Customer report preparation: 1 week waiting
  • Client meeting scheduling: 2 weeks total time
  • Offer preparation: 3 days total time
Impact:
  • Extended sales cycle reduces competitiveness
  • Potential customers may find alternatives
  • Sales representatives handle fewer leads
  • Opportunity cost of missed customers (20%)
Issue 3
High Rate of "Bad" Leads in Digital Process

Inefficient lead qualification:

  • 50% of leads filtered out by algorithm
  • 70% of remaining leads never respond
  • Only 10% of leads with offers convert
Impact:
  • Wasted effort in lead generation
  • Low return on marketing investment
  • Sales time spent on non-converting leads
  • Reduced overall profitability

Proposed Solutions

Solution 1
Unified Information System Implementation

What will change:

  • Add: Single integrated system combining CNTR, OPER, INSU, and AUIN
  • Drop: Separate systems and redundant data entry
Benefits:
  • Reduced error rates (from 15% to 5%)
  • 10% reduction in back office processing time
  • €20,000 annual IT cost savings
  • Increased cross-selling opportunities

BPR Principles: Technology enablement, Integration, Standardization

Solution 2
Hybrid Lead Generation and Qualification

What will change:

  • Add: Advanced lead scoring with traditional sales expertise
  • Add: Pre-qualification step before offer preparation
  • Drop: Separate lead generation processes
Benefits:
  • 20% improvement in "good" lead ratio
  • Increased conversion rates (from 10% to 15%)
  • Reduced non-response rate (from 70% to below 50%)
  • More efficient resource allocation

BPR Principles: Task elimination, Triage, Empower

Solution 3
Streamlined Report and Offer Preparation

What will change:

  • Add: Automated report generation
  • Add: Standardized offer templates
  • Drop: Manual report preparation by sales admin
  • Drop: Separate offer preparation processes
Benefits:
  • Reduced waiting time (from 1 week to near-zero)
  • Faster offer preparation (from 5 hours to 1-2 hours)
  • Improved cycle time efficiency (from 8.5% to 15%)
  • 20% more customers served

BPR Principles: Automation, Case manager, Resequencing

Solution 4
Integrated Contract and Payment Processing

What will change:

  • Add: Unified contract processing workflow
  • Add: Automated payment tracking
  • Drop: Separate back office processes
Benefits:
  • 10% reduction in back office processing time
  • Lower error rates in contract processing
  • Reduced payment investigation time
  • Improved staff utilization

BPR Principles: Case-based work, Integration, Exception handling
